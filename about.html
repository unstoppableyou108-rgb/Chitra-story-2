A Letter From The Studio

Why we started writing placards.

We started Chitra Story because we kept buying paintings that told us nothing. Beautiful pieces, no context — you'd hang them up and forget why you liked them in the first place.

So we began doing one small thing differently: every painting we sell ships with a printed placard, the kind you'd find next to a piece in a gallery. Who made it, what tradition it draws from, why we picked it. Not marketing copy — the actual story.

Most of what you'll find here is hand-finished reproduction work, done by artists we work with directly across studios in Maharashtra and Rajasthan. We size everything for the walls Indian homes actually have, not the oversized lofts you see in catalogues. And if a piece doesn't have a story worth telling, we don't carry it.

If you ever want to talk to a person instead of a form, our number is at the bottom of every page. We read every message ourselves.

— Team Chitra Story
