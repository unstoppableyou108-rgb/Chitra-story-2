Before you buy

Honest answers, no fine print.

How fast does it ship?
Production starts within 24 hours of order. Most pieces are on your wall within 5-7 working days, pan-India.
What comes with the painting?
Every piece ships ready to hang, along with a printed story card describing the idea and the artist behind it.
Can I request a custom size or story?
Yes. Use the custom order form to describe the story you have in mind, and our team will get back within a day.
What's your return policy?
If the piece arrives damaged or isn't what you expected, let us know within 24 hours of delivery and we'll sort it out.
